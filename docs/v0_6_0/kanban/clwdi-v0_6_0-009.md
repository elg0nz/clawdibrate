## Problem

If the bug-identifier can't map failures to a specific AGENTS.md section, the orchestrator should log a warning and continue — not crash with RuntimeError.

## Fix

Log the unmapped failures, skip the implementer step for that iteration, and surface the count in the final report. Track frequency of unmapped failures — if consistently high, the bug-identifier prompt needs refinement.
